**-- SATA (Serial AT Attachment) --**
SATA Revision 1.0
- SATA 1.5 Gbit/s, 1 meter

SATA Revision 2.0
- SATA 3.0 Gbit/s, 1 meter

SATA Revision 3.0
- SATA 6.0 Gbit/s, 1 meter

SATA Revision 3.2
- SATA 16 Gbit/s, 1 meter

eSATA (external SATA)
- Matches the SATA version
- 2 meters
